Why #000000

Contrast
Accentuation of differences between elements. Contrast is important because the meaningful essence of any thing is defined by its value, properties, or quality relative to something else. Nothing has meaning by itself.
Balance
Equal distribution of visual weight. Balance occurs when our eyes require the visual weight to be equal on the two sides of the axis. We are bilateral creatures and our sense of balance is innate.
